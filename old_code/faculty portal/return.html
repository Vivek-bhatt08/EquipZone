Return Policy

Thank you for using our sports equipment issuing service. Please review our return policy to ensure a smooth process.

Return Period

All equipment must be returned within 1 hour of issuance. Faculty members are responsible for checking the condition of the equipment and completing the return process.

Condition of Returned Equipment

The equipment should be returned in the same condition as it was issued. Faculty members will:

  • Inspect the equipment for any damage or wear.
  • Ensure the equipment is clean and functional.
  • Record any issues and take appropriate action.

Damaged or Lost Equipment

Return Process

To return equipment, students should bring it to the designated return location. Faculty members will:

  • Check the condition of the equipment.
  • Update the return status in the system.
  • Apply any late fees or charges for damage if necessary.
