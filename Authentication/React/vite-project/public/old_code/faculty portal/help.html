Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the bottom of the sign in page, fill out the required information, and then submit the form. You account will be created.

How can I borrow sports equipments?

Once logged in (as a student), navigate to the "Check Item" section, browse the available items, and then go to "Issue Item" section and click "Issue" button next to the equipment you want to borrow. Then, the issuing faculty will provide you with the equipment.

How can I issue sports equipments to students?

Once logged in (as a faculty member), navigate to the "Issue New Item" section, enter student's details as per their ID card, click "Submit" to complete the process.

What is the return policy for issued equipment?

All issued equipment must be returned within 1 hour of issuance. Late returns may incur a penalty fee. Please refer to our Return Policy page for more details.

How do I check the availability of equipment?

You can check the availability of equipment in the "Check Item" section after logging in as a student. Each item has a status indicator showing whether it is available, low stock, or out of stock.

What should I do if the equipment is damaged?

If you receive damaged equipment or it gets damaged during use, please report it immediately to the issuing faculty via the "Contact Us" page of your account's dashboard. They can get it changed for you.

Who can I contact for further assistance?

If you need further assistance, you can reach out to our support team via the "Contact Us" page or email us directly at support@equipzone.com.
